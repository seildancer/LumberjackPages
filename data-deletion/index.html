Thank you for using our app. This guide explains how users can delete their data from within the app or by submitting a request.

üîπ Data Users Can Delete Directly In-App

1. Profile Photo

  • Users can change their profile photo directly within the app.
  • When updated, the previous photo is automatically overwritten and removed from the server.

2. Uploaded Videos

  • Users can delete videos they have uploaded by navigating to the [Progress] section.
  • Tap on the video to play it, and tap the settings icon overlayed. Remove button will appear, tap it to remove video.
  • Deleted videos are immediately removed from the app and our servers.

3. Account

  • Users can delete their account by clicking on their username at the top of the main UI and then delete account button.
  • It will wipe all data including user metadata, followships, videos, reactions, profile picture, and weekly summary.

üîπ Data Not Currently Deletable In-App

üîê Data Retention & Deletion Policy
